Marshal Woburn

by Mitchell CampsMay 18th, 2018

PreviousNext

The soft vinyl exterior and the fret grille cloth with signature script logo give it a stage equipment look. Marshall's Woburn Speaker sounds concert-worthy, too, with powerful built-in amps, two dome tweeters, and two sizable woofers. Volume, treble, and bass knobs join a pair of buttons and an old-school rocker switch on the brass top plate, adding to the retro vibe. Inputs include Bluetooth for your phone, RCAs for your turntable, and a 3.5mm and optical for everything in between.
