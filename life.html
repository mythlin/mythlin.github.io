CURTISS ZEUS ELECTRIC MOTORCYCLE

The Zeus concept is the first hint of what Curtiss' all-electric future will look like. Keeping the styling cues of their previous gas-powered models, the Zeus is billed as the world's first e-twin motorcycle.

MARSHALL WOBURN SPEAKER

The soft vinyl exterior and the fret grille cloth with signature script logo give it a stage equipment look. Marshall's Woburn Speaker sounds concert-worthy, too, with powerful built-in amps, two dome tweeters, and two sizable woofers.

J.R's ECO HUT AT KIMO ESTATE

Perched on a hilltop on a farm in Gundagai, J.R's Eco Hut at Kimo Estate offers a swanky way to enjoy the Australian wilderness. The one-room A-frame operates completely off-grid. Although they resemble a tent, the accommodations are anything but rugged.

PLASAN YAGU ARMORED VEHICLE

Plasan is an Israeli manufacturer that provides armor kits for everything from light trucks to aircraft and the US military's MRAPs. Their latest design is the Yagu, based on the Arctic Cat Wildcat.
